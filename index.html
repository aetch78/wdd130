Nevada

Nevada is a state in the western Unites States. It is bordered by California to the west, Oregon to the northwest, Idaho to the northeast, Utah to the east, and Arizona to the southeast. Nevada became the second of two states added to the Union during the Civil War (the first being West Virginia) and became known as the “Battle Born State” as a result of when it achieved statehood. Nevada's harsh but rich environment shaped its history and culture.
