Alexx Echevarria | WDD 130

Hi there! My name is Alexx Echevarria, and it's a pleasure to introduce myself. I am a business owner, currently pursuing my education at BYU Idaho. Outside of work and studies, I find fulfillment in giving back to my community by volunteering for the Nevada Nurses Foundation.

When I'm not busy with my various commitments, you can find me exploring the great outdoors, especially fishing. There's something special about the calmness and tranquility of nature that I find rejuvenating. I believe that time spent in nature is essential for our mental and physical well-being.

I've been fortunate enough to call the Sparks-Reno Nevada area home since 2014, before which I lived in Las Vegas. I've come to love the beauty of the Silver State and all the adventures it has to offer.

Overall, I am someone who enjoys the simple things in life and finds meaning in helping others. I'm always up for a good conversation and look forward to connecting with others who share my passions and interests.

Nevada

Nevada is a state in the western United States. It is bordered by California to the west, Oregon to the northwest, Idaho to the northeast, Utah to the east, and Arizona to the southeast. Nevada became the second of two states added to the Union during the Civil War (the first being West Virginia) and became known as the "Battle Born State" as a result of when it achieved statehood. Nevada's harsh but rich environment shaped its history and culture.
